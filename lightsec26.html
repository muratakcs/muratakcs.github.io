Welcome to LightSec 2026

LightSec 2026 continues the series' tradition of promoting novel research on security, privacy, and trust issues related to lightweight security. As new applications in AI, blockchain, IoT, and 5G/6G become ubiquitous, ensuring security with constrained resources is more critical than ever.

This year, we are excited to host the workshop in the beautiful coastal city of Antalya, at Akdeniz University.

Call For Papers

LightSec 2026 enthusiastically welcomes papers on algorithms, protocols, techniques, and their secure and efficient implementations for applications utilizing advanced cryptographic algorithms.

Topics of Interest

  • Lightweight Cryptography for IoT and Cyber-Physical Systems
  • Post-Quantum Cryptography on Constrained Devices
  • Efficient Zero-Knowledge Proofs & Homomorphic Encryption
  • Hardware Security (FPGA/ASIC implementations)
  • Side-Channel Analysis and Countermeasures
  • Privacy-Preserving AI/ML on Edge Devices

Submission Guidelines

Authors are invited to submit original research papers. All submissions will be double-blind peer-reviewed.
