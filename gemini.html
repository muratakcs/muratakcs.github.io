I am an academic in the Department of Computer Engineering. My research primarily focuses on Applied Cryptography, Network Security, and Privacy-Preserving Technologies.

Currently, I am investigating efficient cryptographic protocols for constrained environments and the intersection of Game Theory and Security. I obtained my Ph.D. in Computer Engineering.

Research Interests

  • Post-Quantum Cryptography
  • Blockchain & Distributed Ledger Technologies
  • Game Theoretic Approaches to Security
  • Secure Multi-party Computation
