bio

Hello. I am an academic in the Department of Computer Engineering. My work exists at the intersection of Cryptography, Game Theory, and Distributed Systems.

I focus on designing secure protocols for the next generation of digital infrastructure. Currently, I am exploring how blockchain incentives can be modeled using game theory to solve real-world problems (such as carbon footprint reduction).
