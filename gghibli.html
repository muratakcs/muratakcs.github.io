A Little About Me

Hello! Like a traveler mapping uncharted lands, I explore the complex landscapes of Applied Cryptography and Network Security. I am an academic in the Department of Computer Engineering.

My goal is to build digital structures that are secure, fair, and resilient, much like the hidden villages found in old tales. I am currently studying how we can use technology to protect privacy and perhaps even help the environment.

Fields of Study

  • Privacy-preserving technologies (keeping secrets safe).
  • Zero-Knowledge Proofs (proving truth without revealing data).
  • Blockchain incentives for sustainability (tech for nature).
  • Game Theoretic approaches to security.
